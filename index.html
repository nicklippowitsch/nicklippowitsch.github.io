Work

Dead Boy Decetcives

VFX Producer

Dubbed the Hardy Boys on acid, Dead Boy Dectives is kaleidoscopic mystery of series full of Devils, Demons, Ghosts and Ghouls. I led an inhouse VFX team of 6, collabrotating with Execs, Producers, Editors and Vendors to help bring the world of the Dead Boys to life through VFX and the visual storytelling language of the world as inpsired by the classic comics.

DOOM PATROL

VFX Producer

A gritty and irreverent take on DC's strangest group of heroes, who band together after suffering horrific accidents that granted them superhuman abilities but left them scarred and disfigured in more ways than one. I led visual effects spotting sessions and discussions of key sequences, as well as creating precise bids encompassing all aspects of visual effects!

Supergirl

VFX Producer

A young woman embraces her destiny as Supergirl, balancing her life as Kara Danvers while protecting National City from powerful threats, proving that being a hero is more than just having superpowers. Communication is my superpower, and on Supergirl I really got the chance to shine by fostering effective communication among senior leadership and key project stakeholders.

Salvation

VFX & Post Production Coordinator

A young MIT student and a tech billionaire uncover a government secret—an asteroid is on a collision course with Earth in six months—forcing them into a high-stakes race against time to prevent global catastrophe. Coordinated cross-border communication between the Vancouver VFX department and LA-based Post and Showrunners to support creative alignment and timely delivery.

Hot Date

VFX Producer & Post Production Coordinator

Hot Date is a comedy series that follows real-life couple Emily Axford and Brian Murphy as they navigate the awkward, absurd, and often disastrous world of modern dating, relationships, and social interactions—all with a sharp, satirical edge. I helped established visual effects workflows and created our shot tracking database. I also orchestrated the notes process, between executive producers and visual effect venders, with clear communication of constructive, actionable notes.
